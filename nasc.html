NEHRU ARTS AND SCIENCE COLLEGE KANHANGAD

About Us

Nehru Arts and Science College is also known as Nehru College,Kanhangad is a post graduate and graduate college in Kanhangad,established in 1968, it is accredited from NAAC and it is affiliated to Kannur University. A dedicated and learned team set up an educational society namely The Nehru Memorial Educational Society in 1966 under the distinguished presidentship of the late Sri. C. K. Nair following which the society was duly registered.
